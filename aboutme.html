About Me

Hi there! My name is Matej Sefcik and I'm a 22 year old photographer and filmmaker based in New Jersey. I am originally from Slovakia, but my parents emigrated to the U.S. when I was very young and I've lived in New Jersey ever since.


When I was a little kid, I loved to record home videos with my friends. Little did I know, it would turn into something I would end up pursuing as a career. It wasn't until college when I discovered what I truly loved, and that was being behind the camera, and I ended up graduating from Seton Hall University with a degree in Visual/Sound Media with a concentration in Film Production.


Not only do I love film, but my interest for photography grew as I gained more experience being behind the camera. I love to photograph anything that interests me, but my main focus is in cities and landscapes.


Aside from my life behind the camera, I am an ice hockey and tennis fanatic, I've been playing guitar since I was 6, and I love to ski. Traveling and meeting new people along the way is a huge passion of mine as well.


I can create work in a range of different styles and I am confident I can provide a great finished product to a client, whether it be a small photoshoot or a large-scale video project. With that said, I hope to work with you soon!

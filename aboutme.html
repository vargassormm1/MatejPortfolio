About Me

My name is Matej Sefcik and I am a 22 year old photographer and videographer based in New Jersey. I've always had a passion for documenting my life through photos and videos but it wasn't until recently I decided it was the career I wanted to pursue. I have a degree in Visual and Sound Media with a concentration in Film Production from Seton Hall University. My videography can be found on my Instagram @matsefcik as well as other content. I hope to work with you soon.
